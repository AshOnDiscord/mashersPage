About Us

Welcome! We are the MASH Peer Leaders. We are a small group of students and leaders trying to make technology easier and more accessible for education. Technology is sometimes difficult and confusing, so we strive to do out best to simplify and utilize it in fun and educational ways.

Did you know? MASH stands for Machine and Software Helpers.
